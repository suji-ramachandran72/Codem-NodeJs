AIM : 
To write the synopsis for the story titled “When the Two Worlds Meet.” 
To understand the theme, characters and message of the story. 
STUDY : 
A synopsis is a short summary that explains the main idea of a story. It describes the characters, 
important events and the message conveyed through the story. Writing a synopsis helps in 
organizing the storyline clearly before creating a video project. 
SYNOPSIS: 
WHEN THE TWO WORLDS MEET 
Introduction: 
“When the Two Worlds Meet” is a story about two girls from different backgrounds who meet 
in the city and develop a meaningful friendship. The story highlights the contrast between village 
life and city life. 
Kaviya’s Struggle: 
Kaviya is a girl from a small village who comes to the city searching for a job because her 
father is sick and her family needs financial support. During her first interview she struggles to 
speak English confidently and some people laugh at her accent. This makes her feel embarrassed 
and discouraged. 
Meeting Isha: 
During this difficult moment, Kaviya meets Isha, a modern city girl who spends most of her 
time using her smartphone. At first she appears rude, but later she notices Kaviya practicing 
interview questions alone and understands her situation. 
Friendship and Support: 
• Isha decides to help Kaviya improve her confidence. 
• She teaches her how to answer interview questions. 
• She also shows her how to use technology for learning. 
Slowly their friendship grows stronger. 
Important Moment: 
One night a power cut leaves them without internet or mobile phones. They spend time talking 
and sharing memories. At that moment they realize that real human connection is stronger than 
digital communication. 
Conclusion: 
With the confidence gained from Isha’s help, Kaviya attends another interview and finally 
gets the job. She thanks Isha for supporting her during a difficult time. 
RESULT : 
Thus the synopsis for the story “When the Two Worlds Meet” is written successfully..
